### Philosophy

Nabeel's approach to AI development centers on the principle that "Agentic systems don't just respond. They act with purpose." This philosophy drives his work in building AI agents that exhibit genuine agency - the ability to independently pursue objectives, adapt to changing conditions, and execute complex multi-step plans.

### Connection to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

The principles of agentic AI development are directly applicable to physical AI and humanoid robotics. Just as digital agents must perceive their environment, reason about their goals, and execute actions, physical robots must do the same in the real world. The transition from virtual agents to embodied systems represents the natural evolution of agentic AI - extending autonomous decision-making and goal pursuit from digital environments to the physical realm.

This textbook reflects Nabeel's expertise in building goal-driven systems, applying those principles to the domain of embodied intelligence where agents must navigate, interact with, and adapt to the complexities of the physical world through robotic systems.
